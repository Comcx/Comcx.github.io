2. Prediction vs Abstraction

Let's review our example above: He is a Magic

Well, is it means he is absolutly a Magic? No, it's just a statement. We now call this kind of sentence Prediction. More examples:

  • She is pretty.
  • Yogami is interesting. ...

Strictly, we use '=' only when a sentence is a prediction.

However, then what is abstraction?? Actually, we call = abstraction above, therefore, we need a new symbol to represent abstraction. Here we choose ':'. Let me list some examples of abstractions:

  • A smart boy
  • An interesting program
  • The boy who won ...

It is interesting that the sentence A smart boy can also be rewritten as the boy who is smart.
